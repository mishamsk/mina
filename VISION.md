# Mina's Vision

- A full accounting data model that can express arbitrarily complex transactions.
- As easy to use as a consumer personal finance tool: simplified by default, with full accounting detail available when needed.
- Cover household money end to end: record and reconcile transactions; track cash, bank accounts, currencies—including crypto used as currency—exchange rates, and personal debts; plan with budgets, reports, and forecasts.
- Stop short of investment management: investments may be represented as periodically updated account values with gain/loss adjustments, but Mina does not model securities or mirror brokerage portfolios.
- Great UX that is useful, clear, and fun for humans.
- Super snappy at household scale.
- Automation beyond data integrations: AI-driven classification, reconciliation, workflows, and financial insights.
- Hackable by agents and humans through REST, MCP, and CLI interfaces.
- Local-first and portable as a single database file.
- Built for one household—not as a multi-user SaaS.
